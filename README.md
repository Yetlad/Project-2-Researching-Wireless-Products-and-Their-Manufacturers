# Wireless Products & Manufacturers Research (2026)

##  Overview
This repository contains web research on key wireless products used in modern IoT and wireless communication systems.  
For each product,
one manufacturer was identified (not distributors/resellers), 
a direct link to the manufacturer’s product page, and 
a short 2-sentence description.

---

## Wireless Products, Manufacturers, Links, and Descriptions

### 1) LoRaWAN Gateway
**Manufacturer:** RAKwireless  
**Product Page Link:** https://www.rakwireless.com/en-us/products/lpwan-gateways-and-concentrators/rak7243  
**Description:** The RAK7243C is a LoRaWAN gateway designed to connect long-range IoT sensors to a network using LoRaWAN technology. It supports multiple backhaul options such as Ethernet, Wi-Fi, and cellular for flexible deployment.

---

### 2) Zigbee Module
**Manufacturer:** Nordic Semiconductor  
**Product Page Link:** https://www.nordicsemi.com/Products/Wireless/WiFi  
**Description:** Nordic Semiconductor produces wireless SoCs that support Zigbee for low-power mesh networking in smart home and industrial IoT applications. These solutions are optimized for energy efficiency and provide strong ecosystem support for embedded development.

---

### 3) Wi-Fi 6 Chipset
**Manufacturer:** Nordic Semiconductor  
**Product Page Link:** https://www.nordicsemi.com/Products/Wireless/WiFi  
**Description:** Nordic’s Wi-Fi 6 solutions enable fast and reliable 802.11ax connectivity for IoT devices requiring higher throughput and improved efficiency. The chipset is designed for low power operation and works well in multiprotocol wireless systems.

---

### 4) NB-IoT Module
**Manufacturer:** Sequans Communications  
**Product Page Link:** https://sequans.com/products/  
**Description:** Sequans develops NB-IoT modules for low-power wide-area connectivity used in smart metering, tracking, and remote monitoring. These modules are built for long battery life and reliable cellular coverage.

---

### 5) Sigfox Device
**Manufacturer:** Ezurio  
**Product Page Link:** https://www.ezurio.com  
**Description:** Ezurio manufactures IoT wireless modules and devices that support low-power long-range communication technologies such as Sigfox. These products are commonly used in industrial monitoring and asset tracking applications where power efficiency is important.

---

### 6) Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) Beacon
**Manufacturer:** Nordic Semiconductor  
**Product Page Link:** https://www.nordicsemi.com/Products/Wireless/WiFi  
**Description:** Nordic’s BLE-capable SoCs are widely used to build BLE beacons for proximity detection, indoor navigation, and tracking. They are known for low power consumption and strong wireless performance.

---

### 7) Ultra-Wideband (UWB) Transceiver
**Manufacturer:** Ezurio  
**Product Page Link:** https://www.ezurio.com  
**Description:** Ezurio provides UWB solutions that enable high-accuracy ranging and positioning for real-time location systems (RTLS). UWB transceivers are ideal for applications requiring precise distance measurement and secure proximity detection.

---

### 8) Wi-Fi 6E Access Point
**Manufacturer:** SparkLAN (Wi-Fi 6/6E solution provider/manufacturer)  
**Product Page Link:** https://www.sparklan.com/powerful-capable-tri-band-wi-fi-6-6e-solution/  
**Description:** SparkLAN offers Wi-Fi 6E tri-band solutions that support the 6 GHz band for improved speed and reduced interference. This makes Wi-Fi 6E access points suitable for high-density environments requiring low latency and high throughput.

---

### 9) 5G IoT Module
**Manufacturer:** Sequans Communications  
**Product Page Link:** https://sequans.com/products/  
**Description:** Sequans develops cellular IoT modules that support 5G and LTE connectivity for modern connected devices. These modules provide high data rates, improved latency, and scalable connectivity for industrial and smart applications.
